FAQs

WHAT IS GAMFO?

GAMFO is a fantasy esports and tournament hosting platform where fans like yourself can play Fantasy games and esports tournaments based on your esports knowledge and skill.

WHAT IS FANTASY ESPORTS ON GAMFO?

Fantasy is a game of skill and your esports knowledge. You chose your team from all the players/teams playing in PUBG, Valorant, FreeFire, CODM, CS:Go and other esports games. What are you waiting for, come participate, play and win! Here are easy steps to get started.

HOW TO PLAY FANTASY ESPORTS ON GAMFO?
  1. Select a Match: Select a Match on any ongoing series of a game
  2. Select a Contest: Select a contest you want to participate in
  3. Create your Team: Create a team with your esports knowledge and skill and flaunt to your friends
  4. Follow the Match: Track the match in the Live Matches or My Entry section
  5. Withdraw your winnings: Withdraw your winnings in your wallet.
WHEN DO I GET MY WINNINGS?

The winner is declared after the completion of the match and the winnings are credited to your GAMFO account usually within a couple of hours after the match is completed.
